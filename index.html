How to use this site (quick)

  1. Pick one topic page.
  2. Choose 1–2 artist references (don’t copy — analyse what makes them effective).
  3. Complete the “Starter tasks” to generate your own primary studies.
  4. Develop 2–3 compositions and test media/techniques.
  5. Build a coherent final outcome plan.
